Our Programs

Holistic care for the whole child.

Education Support

Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. We provide:

  • Scholastic materials (books, pens, uniforms)
  • Tuition support for vulnerable children
  • After-school homework clubs
Support a Student

Nutrition & Feeding

A hungry child cannot learn. We ensure that the children in our care receive nutritious meals, often the only substantial meal they get in a day.

Feed a Child

Safe Play & Mentorship

We provide a safe haven away from the streets where children can play, express themselves, and receive counseling. Our university student partners serve as mentors and role models.
