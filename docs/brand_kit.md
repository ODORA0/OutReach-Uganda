## ðŸ”¤ Brand Fonts

We use fonts that are clean, modern, but have a human touch.

### Headline Font
*   **Family**: **Outfit** (Google Font)
*   **Weights**: Bold (700), SemiBold (600)
*   **Usage**: Page titles, section headers.
*   **Feel**: Modern, geometric but friendly, highly legible.

### Body Text Font
*   **Family**: **Inter** (Google Font)
*   **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500)
*   **Usage**: Paragraphs, navigation, buttons.
*   **Feel**: Clean, neutral, highly readable on all screens.

### Accent / CTA Font (Optional)
*   **Family**: **Caveat** (Google Font)
*   **Weights**: Regular (400)
*   **Usage**: Short testimonials, "handwritten" notes, emphasis.
*   **Feel**: Personal, human, authentic.

## ðŸŽ­ Brand Mood & Art Direction

*   **Keywords**: Hopeful, Uplifting, Dignified, Community-Rooted, Warm.
*   **Photography**:
    *   Focus on **smiles and connection**, not poverty or despair.
    *   Warm lighting (golden hour).
    *   Candid shots of children playing, learning, or interacting with mentors.
    *   Avoid "savior" angles; shoot at eye level with the children.
*   **Iconography**:
    *   Simple, rounded strokes.
    *   Use the "Savanna Gold" or "Kivulu Clay" for icons.
*   **Textures**:
    *   Subtle organic patterns (e.g., fabric weaves, topographic lines) in the background to add depth without clutter.
