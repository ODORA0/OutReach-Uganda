Our Story

Rooted in the community, driven by compassion.

Why Makerere–Kivulu?

Makerere–Kivulu is a vibrant community, but one that faces significant challenges. Many families struggle with poverty, food insecurity, and a lack of educational opportunities.

We started Outreach Uganda because we saw children with immense potential but no platform to express it. We believe that geography should not dictate destiny.

Our Core Values

Dignity

We treat every child and family with the respect they deserve, focusing on empowerment, not pity.

Community

We work *with* the community, not just *for* them. Local leaders and parents are key partners.

Transparency

We are accountable for every donation, ensuring it goes directly to the needs of the children.
